Our team has worked very hard to develop the operating system, and we're happy to show you the compoundOS.

The Boot Screen.

Your journey starts from booting up a computer, so we designed to make the boot animation look nice.

The Login Screen.

We prompt you to a nice login screen after you boot, and "Welcome back." You can either enter your password to access all your data, or to shutdown / restart put your computer to sleep.

Start Exploring.

You can pick an app to launch, or check the latest news with widgets all at once. You can even customize whatever you want in the menu!

Quick Controls.

Need to access the settings like display brightness or sound? We got you covered. The quick settings allows you to do many kinds of settings quickly. You can customize the quick settings tiles too!

Can Run Any Apps.

The system is designed to be able to run any types of application, from Windows, macOS, Linux, etc.. It just works!
